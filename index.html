Corporate Profile

Our Legacy

Founded in the late 1970s and formally incorporated on November 25, 1987, Miki Wire Works Pvt Ltd has grown from a visionary enterprise into a cornerstone of India's industrial sector. Headquartered in Ranchi, Jharkhand, strictly adhering to principles of integrity and excellence.


Group Structure

The Miki Group operates through 4 strategic units to ensure comprehensive Pan-India logistics coverage and service delivery.
